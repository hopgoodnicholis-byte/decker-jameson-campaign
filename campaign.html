Meet the Candidates

Lucifer Decker

Lucifer Decker is a leader dedicated to strengthening the connection between government and the people it serves. He believes leadership should be built on trust, accountability, and the willingness to face problems directly. With a strong commitment to the community, Decker has made it clear that his priority is creating a government that listens to its citizens and works to solve the challenges they face every day.

Peter Jameson

Peter Jameson is a leader committed to restoring trust between the government and the people it serves. Throughout his career, he has believed that strong leadership begins with listening to the community and acting in the best interest of its citizens. His dedication to public service comes from a deep belief that government should be transparent, accountable, and focused on real solutions.

Our Plan for Los Santos

  • Mandating Better LEO Training – Expanding and strengthening training requirements for law enforcement officers so they are better prepared to protect the community. This ensures officers have the knowledge, discipline, and tools needed to respond to modern challenges.
  • Removing illegals – Addressing illegal immigration through stronger enforcement and coordination with authorities. The goal is to maintain the rule of law while protecting jobs and resources for legal residents.
  • Bi-monthly stimulus checks – Providing regular financial relief to citizens to help support families and stimulate the local economy. These payments aim to keep money circulating within the community.
  • Mass warrant distribution and execution – Aggressively pursuing outstanding warrants to reduce crime and increase accountability. By clearing unresolved cases, the justice system can operate more efficiently.
  • More housing for the city and county – Expanding housing developments across Los Santos and Blaine County. This initiative focuses on making housing more available and affordable for residents.
  • Face-to-Face conversations with officials – Holding regular town halls and community meetings so citizens can directly speak with their leaders. Government should remain accessible and responsive to the people.
  • Problem solving streamline – Cutting unnecessary bureaucracy so government can respond faster to issues. Streamlining decision-making allows problems to be solved more efficiently.
  • Lowering taxes – Reducing the financial burden placed on working citizens and small businesses. Lower taxes help encourage economic growth and allow residents to keep more of their earnings.
  • Removing inactive officials – Ensuring leadership positions are held by individuals actively working to improve the community. Government should not tolerate inactivity or lack of accountability.
  • Gang control initiatives – Developing stronger strategies to combat gang activity and organized crime. This includes both law enforcement action and community programs aimed at prevention.
  • Education support for low-income families – Expanding access to educational programs, job training, and learning resources. Education is key to breaking cycles of poverty and creating long-term opportunity.
  • Accessible healthcare for all citizens – Working toward healthcare systems that provide care to every resident regardless of income. Citizens should never have to choose between their health and financial stability.
